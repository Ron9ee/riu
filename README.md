## What is Riu?

**Riu is a DIY desk robot that helps you work, focus, and learn.**  
It's like a to-do app — but physical, local, and full of personality.

Riu lives on your desk, connects to your computer, and helps with small daily things like:
- Managing your to-do list
- Running Pomodoro timers
- Setting reminders (like "drink water every 30 mins")
- Making faces, moving around, and being silly
- Talking to you (coming soon!)
